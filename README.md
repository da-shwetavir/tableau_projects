# 🧮EarlyON Child and Family Centres Across Toronto 
## 📊Project Overview
EarlyON Child and Family Centres provide free programs for parents, caregivers, and children aged 0–6 years. This project analyzes the geographic distribution and program availability of EarlyON centres to understand service accessibility

## 🗄️Dataset
[Source: Public EarlyON Child & Family Centres dataset](https://open.toronto.ca/dataset/earlyon-child-and-family-centres/) \
Main variables include:
- Ward Name
- Address
- Latitude & Longitude
- Agency Name
- Languages Offered
- French Language Programs
- Indigenous Programs
- Located in School
## 🧐Key Insights
- Most EarlyON centres are concentrated in specific wards.
- A small no. of offer French-language programs.
- Many centres operate within community centers, buildings  rather than in schools.
